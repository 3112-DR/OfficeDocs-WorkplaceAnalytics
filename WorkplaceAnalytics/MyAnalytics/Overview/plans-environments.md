## Support of environments

### Office 365 environments that are supported
* Worldwide Multi-tenant
* Dedicated Multi-tenant
* Go-locals
* GCC
 
### Office 365 environments that are not supported
* GCC-High
* DoD
* Gallatin
* BlackForest
